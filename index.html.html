Creating an AI Agents HR and Marketplace

The Problem

The AI agent market is exploding ($13B by 2030), but there's a critical disconnect:

  • 77% of small businesses lack the knowledge to adopt AI
  • Small businesses miss out on cost savings (e.g., 50% scheduling time reduction)
  • Businesses need specialized AI agents but have no centralized marketplace to find, hire, buy,or sell them
  • Developers build powerful agents but don't have avenues to monetize them
  • Non-technical creators with great ideas lack the skills to create agents and earn income

Our Solution

NeoScout is the Indeed for AI agents

We've built a three-sided marketplace where:

  • Small Businesses post jobs to hire pre-vetted AI agents—reducing labor costs with no technical knowledge required
  • Developers list pre-vetted agents to be hired and monetize their skills
  • Creators submit ideas, get paired with developers, and earn via our revenue-sharing model (80% to creators)
